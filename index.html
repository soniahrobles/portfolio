Layered Realities

Layered Realities explores the interconnected systems of nature, humanity, and machine through a light-reactive, three-layered painting. Each layer(cyan, magenta, and yellow) reveals itself under red, green, or blue LED light, allowing different narratives to emerge depending on the illumination. The work is framed by 3D-printed vines and flowers, with laser-cut mechanical elements embedded into the painting itself, blending organic and industrial materials. This fusion of handcraft and digital fabrication reflects our shifting relationship with media technologies. Responding to the course’s theme of perception and mediated experience, the piece uses light as a metaphor for how technology filters reality. AI tools and digital design processes shaped the structure and visual logic of the work, making the act of "revealing" both visual and conceptual.
